Install TorqueShed

Use TorqueShed like a native app. Faster access, full-screen experience, and a home screen icon.

TorqueShed is installed on your device.

Add TorqueShed to Your Device

Follow the steps for your browser to install TorqueShed as an app.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  3. Tap "Install" to confirm

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner

Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Look for the install icon in the address bar (right side)
  2. Or open the browser menu and select "Install TorqueShed"
  3. Click "Install" to confirm
